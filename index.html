Dalgona Coffee Recipe

This yummy South Korean coffee thats all over famous in the world and requires 4 ingredients-all of which you propably have in your cupboard-you feel you have won a jackpot

INGREDIENTS:

  • MILK
  • SUGAR
  • INSTANT COFFEE OR EXPRESSO Granules
  • HOT WATER

    • DALGONA MAKING PROCESS

Step:1-Combine the instant coffee,sugar and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons of instant coffee and 2 tablespoons of sugar and 2 tablespoons of hot water to medium mixing bowls

Step:2-Whisk,whisk,whisk until the coffee becomes tasty and fluffy.
Using an electric hand-held mixer

Step:3-Serve with milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
